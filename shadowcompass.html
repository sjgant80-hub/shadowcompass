How ShadowCompass works

The short version.

A private shadow-work practice in your browser. Three layers — what's free, what's Pro, and where the AI fits in.

1 · The free reading is genuinely useful

Shadow Mirror, Pattern Detector, Dream Decoder, and Projection Checker are not feature-limited demos. They are full, complete tools running curated Jungian readings on your input. Use them indefinitely. Your shadow journal builds locally as you go.

2 · Pro adds depth + dialogue

Four extra tools — the 21-question Deep Profile, dialogue with the archetypes themselves, a longitudinal individuation map, and a daily 90-second practice. The Deep Profile and Active Imagination tools use AI to compose a reading specifically for you, in your voice, with depth a quiz can't reach.

3 · The AI is plug-in · choose your engine

ShadowCompass is engine-agnostic. The Pro tools work with whichever AI you prefer:

  • Anthropic Claude — best for nuanced shadow work · paid (cheapest depth)
  • Google Gemini — frontier-quality · free tier, no card
  • Groq — fastest readings, free tier · Llama 3.3 70B
  • OpenAI GPT-4o — familiar voice · paid
  • OpenRouter — 30+ models via one key · pay-as-you-go
  • Mistral — privacy-forward EU host · free tier

You paste the key in Settings · it stays in your browser only · the app talks directly to the provider. Or pay for the ShadowCompass Pro licence and we'll handle the AI for you.

4 · Your data is yours

Everything you write — your readings, your dreams, your projection notes, your dialogues with the archetypes — saves to your browser's private storage. Nothing is uploaded to ShadowCompass. Nothing is shared. You can export the whole journal as a JSON file anytime and take it with you.

Get a free AI key · ~90 seconds

Pick a provider. Paste the key.

ShadowCompass works with whichever AI you bring. These three providers offer free tiers good enough to run the Pro tools — no credit card required.

① Google Gemini · most generous free tier RECOMMENDED

1,500 requests per day. Frontier-class model (Gemini 2.5 Flash). No credit card needed.

  1. Go to aistudio.google.com/app/apikey
  2. Sign in with any Google account
  3. Click "Create API key" · pick "Create API key in new project"
  4. Copy the key (starts with AIza…)
  5. Paste it in Settings → AI key with provider set to Google Gemini

② Groq · fastest by far

300+ tokens per second. Llama 3.3 70B. Free tier · no card.

  1. Go to console.groq.com/keys
  2. Sign in (Google / GitHub / email)
  3. Click "Create API Key" · name it "ShadowCompass"
  4. Copy the key (starts with gsk_…)
  5. Paste it in Settings → AI key with provider set to Groq

③ OpenRouter · 30+ models in one key

Free models available. $1 minimum top-up unlocks all major paid models.

  1. Go to openrouter.ai/keys
  2. Sign in · click "Create Key"
  3. Copy the key (starts with sk-or-…)
  4. Paste in Settings → AI key with provider set to OpenRouter

Already paying for ChatGPT or Claude?

Use those keys directly. ShadowCompass supports OpenAI (key from platform.openai.com/api-keys) and Anthropic (key from console.anthropic.com). Both require a credit card on file.

Your key stays in your browser only · the app talks directly to the provider · nothing routes through ShadowCompass.
